Questions Parents Ask

Clear answers before you visit.

A few practical details so the first conversation can focus on your child's needs.

Is the demo class really free?

Yes. The first demo class is free and there is no obligation to join afterwards. It is meant to help the student experience the teaching style before parents decide.

How do you decide the right batch?

We look at the student's class, board, subject needs, current confidence level, and school schedule. If a student needs extra support, we suggest the batch where they will receive the right pace and attention.

Do parents receive progress updates?

Yes. Parents receive monthly updates covering test scores, weak chapters, attendance, and improvement trends. We also message sooner if a student needs attention in a particular topic.

Can students ask doubts outside class?

Yes. Students can send doubts on WhatsApp, and we answer them before the next class wherever possible. Dedicated doubt sessions are also arranged when a chapter needs deeper revision.

Which boards do you cover?

We cover CBSE, ICSE, and Maharashtra State Board for Classes 6-12, with board-pattern tests and previous-year paper practice as exams approach.
